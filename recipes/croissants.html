Croissants recipe

Description

Delicious sweets that you can share with your friends and family. Serve them with some hot chocolate and you will make your host cheer.

Takes a lot of effort to make but it's not the ending... it's the process :)

Ingredients

  • 1kg of flour
  • 1/3 of water for each kilo of flour
  • 0.5Kg of butter
  • 0.3Kg of sugar and salt
  • A few eggs

Steps

  1. First, prepare the fish stock. It's easier if you buy it.
  2. Then, chop all the ingredients.
  3. Once your pan is hot, throw the shrimps and the cuttlefish. Sear them.
  4. Chop the onion and garlic cloves and add them to the pan.
  5. Add the tomato sauce until it reduces.
  6. Add the fish stock. When it starts boiling, add the noodles.
  7. Wait 20 minutes and serve.
