Fideua recipe

Description

Delicious meal to enjoy with your family and friends. It's easy to cook and with a few bucks you can make people happy

This is a traditional Spanish meal that takes about an hour, or an hour and a half, to prepare. It is usually served with a glass of wine.

Ingredients

  • Fish stock
  • 3 shrimps per person
  • 1kg of cuttlefish
  • An onion and a few garlic cloves
  • 200g of tomato sauce
  • Salt and saufron
  • 1kg of noodles

Steps

  1. First, prepare the fish stock. It's easier if you buy it.
  2. Then, chop all the ingredients.
  3. Once your pan is hot, throw the shrimps and the cuttlefish. Sear them.
  4. Chop the onion and garlic cloves and add them to the pan.
  5. Add the tomato sauce until it reduces.
  6. Add the fish stock. When it starts boiling, add the noodles.
  7. Wait 20 minutes and serve.
